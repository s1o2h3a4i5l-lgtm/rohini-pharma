About Concor 5 Tablet 

Concor 5 Tablet is used to treat hypertension (high blood pressure) and angina pectoris. This increases the heart’s workload, and if it continues for a long time, the heart and arteries may not function properly. 

Concor 5 Tablet contains Bisoprolol, which affects the body’s response to some nerve impulses, particularly in the heart. As a result, Concor 5 Tablet slows down the heart rate and makes it easier for the heart to pump blood around the body. Thereby, Concor 5 Tablet helps lower the raised blood pressure and reduces the risk of having a stroke, a heart attack, other heart problems, or kidney problems in the future. 

Concor 5 Tablet should be taken as directed by the physician. Concor 5 Tablet can cause side effects like tiredness, weakness, nausea, vomiting, diarrhoea, or constipation. Most of these side effects of Concor 5 Tablet do not require medical attention and gradually resolve over time. However, if these side effects persist longer, please consult your doctor. 

If you are pregnant or breastfeeding, you should avoid taking Concor 5 Tablet . Concor 5 Tablet is not recommended in children. Before taking Concor 5 Tablet , inform your doctor about your medical history and all medications you are currently taking to rule out any potential side effects and interactions.
