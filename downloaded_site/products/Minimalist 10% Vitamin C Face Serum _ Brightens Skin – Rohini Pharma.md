[Description)]
Minimalist Vitamin C 10% + AG 1% Face Serum is a glow-boosting daily serum packaged with the goodness of vitamin C that helps manage uneven skin tones, sun damage and dull skin with dark spots. Formulated with acetyl glucosamine, this serum provides skin moisturisation and boosts natural hyaluronic acid synthesis. 

Key Ingredients 

- 10% pure ethyl ascorbic acid 

- 1% AG, an amino-monosaccharide (simple sugar) and a precursor of hyaluronic acid 

- Centella water 

- Centella Asiatica Leaf Water, 3-O-Ethyl Ascorbic Acid, Ethoxydiglycol, Dimethyl Isosorbide, Glycerin, Sodium Gluconate, Acetyl Glucosamine, Sodium Hyaluronate, Pullulan, Hydroxyethylcellulose, Xanthan Gum, Sclerotium Gum, Phenoxyethanol, Ethylhexylglycerin, Lecithin, Lactic Acid 

Key Benefits 

- A potent, stable vitamin C formula: This serum is made with a stable vitamin C derivative, 10% ethyl ascorbic acid, closest to pure vitamin C (L-ascorbic acid) in molecular size and weight. With a similar molecule size and 86% pure vitamin C content, it delivers maximum benefits of vitamin C without getting bad or losing efficacy till the last drop (unlike other vitamin C serums) 

- Natural glow booster: This serum ensures high vitamin C delivery directly into your skin. Vitamin C reduces melanin production resulting in an even lighter skin tone. It is also a powerful antioxidant that reduces sun damage and oxidative stress for naturally glowing skin 

- Soothes and hydrates: Formulated with centella water that soothes and calms skin, making this formula non-irritating and suitable for all skin types. 1% acetyl glucosamine added in this serum boosts your hydration and natural exfoliation for radiant plum skin 

- For all skin types: This serum is suitable for all skin types (dry, oily or normal). With anti-inflammatory centella water, it is ideal for even sensitive skin 

- Clean and transparent beauty: This serum is free from fragrance, silicones, sulphates, parabens, essential oils and dyes. Also, it is non-comedogenic, oil-free and hypoallergenic. It is formulated at pH 3.9 – 4.9 formulated with high-grade COS-VCE (Ethyl Ascorbic Acid) by CosMol Korea, a leading global supplier 

Good to Know: 

- No irritant, oil-free and hypoallergenic and non-comedogenic 

- Dermatologically tested and safe for skin 

- Free from fragrance, silicone, sulphates, paraben, dyes and essential oils 

Best Suited For: 
All skin types (dry/normal, oily/combination, acne prone, and sensitive). 

Ideal For: 
Men/Women 

Product Form: 
Liquid. 

Container Type: 
Glass bottle with dropper. 

Area of Application 
Face and neck. 

Concern 

- Beneficial for sun damaged skin 

- Dull skin, dark spots, uneven skin tone, and loss of elasticity 

Directions for Use 

- After cleansing and toning your face, take 2-3 drops of Vitamin C serum on your fingertips with a dropper 

- Apply it on the face by gently tapping it on the skin. With a circular motion, evenly spread the serum and allow your skin to absorb it 

Safety Information 

- Read the label instruction carefully before use 

- Store in a cool and dry place, away from sunlight 

- Keep out of reach of children
