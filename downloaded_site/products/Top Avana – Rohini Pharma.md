## Product Description 

Avanafil + Dapoxetine tablets with brand name Top Avana Tablets is a combination of avanafil (strong medicine) and dapoxetine (slight antidepressant). 

## Uses: 

Avanafil + Dapoxetine tablets are an innovative drug that is used for the treatment of premature ejaculation. Various uses of the medicine are: 

- For treating erectile dysfunction in male - To reduce the risk of premature ejaculation. 



## Dosage: 

Dosage of Top Avana tablets varies depending upon the tolerance of an individual and response to the first dose. It is recommended to give lowest possible dose initially. 

- One tablet 30 minutes before sexual activity. - Do not take it in minute or excessive quantity. - In case of overdose consult your doctor. 



## Side effects: 

Side effects caused by Avanafil + Dapoxetine tablets do not require any medical attention but consult your doctor immediately if you experience adverse effects. 

- Nausea - Vomiting - Sweating - Pain in chest - High or low blood pressure - Pain in jaw - Changes in vision or loos of vision for sometime - Drowsiness - Shortness of breath - Fever - Erection that is painful and can last for more than 4 hours - Allergic reaction such as swelling in hands and legs 



## Precautions and measures: 

Although the side effects do not cause any major problem but certain precautionary measures to be taken while using Top Avana tablets are: 

- Inform your doctor if you are allergic to any of the active ingredients of the medicine. - Inform your doctor if you suffer from any heart disease as the drug can increase chest pain. - If you are taking any nitrates, tell your doctor. - Do not consume the medicine if you are using any other medicine for the same function. - Inform your doctor if you are on other medications such as alpha-blocker or ketoconazole. - Tell your doctor if you suffer from severe depression. - The medicine causes drowsiness. Do not drive or operate heavy machines while on the medication. - Inform your doctor if have any eye problem as the drug may make the symptoms worse. - Inform your doctor if you suffer high or low blood pressure. 



## Benefits: 

Benefits of Avanafil + Dapoxetine tablets are: 

- Increases the blood flow in the penis to keep it in erected position. - The medicine does not require any extra effort for storage. - Can be used for treating premature ejaculation. - The drug does not have any serious side effect. 



## Storage: 

Storage conditions of Top Avana tablets are: 

- Store the medicine at room temperature (20-25 degree centigrade) - Keep away from direct light and moisture. - Keep away from the reach of children. 

Do not use if expired.
