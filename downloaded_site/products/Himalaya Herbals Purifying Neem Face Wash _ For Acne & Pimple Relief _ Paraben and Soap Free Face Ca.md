Himalaya Herbals Purifying Neem Face Wash is a soap-free, herbal formulation that cleans impurities and helps clear pimples. Key Benefits: 

- Neem has antibacterial properties that improve general health and immunity and reduce bacterial infections like acne, boils, and ulcers 

- Neem tackles bacteria from the root and prevents the recurrence of pimples and blemishes 

- Turmeric has antiseptic properties and aids in healing 

- Turmeric helps to even out the skin tone and complexion and increases skin elasticity 

- Turmeric also has anti-inflammatory properties that soothe the skin 


Good to Know: 
It is a herbal product. 

Best Suited For: Oily and acne-prone skin Product Form: Gel 

Area of Application: Facial application Concern: Extreme oily skin 

Directions For Use: 

- Moisten the face 

- Apply a small quantity of the face wash 

- Work up a lather using circular motions 

- Wash off and pat dry 

- Use twice daily 

Safety Information: 

- Read the label carefully before use 

- Store in a cool and dry place 

- Keep out of reach of children 

Quick Tips:
