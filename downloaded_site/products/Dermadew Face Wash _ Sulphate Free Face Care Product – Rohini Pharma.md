[Description)]
Dermadew Face Wash is a gentle sulphate-free natural cleanser combined with skin brightening actives of natural origin and fortified with specific emollients, moisturisers, and skin nourishers. This face care product makes the skin smooth, soft, supple, and refreshed. 

Key Ingredients: 
Purified Water, Capric Acid, Myristic Acid, Potassium Hydroxide, Stearic Acid, Decyl Glucoside, Lauryl Glucoside, Sodium Cocoamphoacetate, Propanediol, Pea Extract, Hydroxyethyl Cellulose, DMDM Hydantoin, Perfume, Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice, Disodium EDTA 

Key Benefits: 

- It can help remove excess sebum from the skin 

- It nourishes the skin and maintains skin health 

- It keeps the skin moisturised, soft, and smooth 

- It brightens and refreshes the skin 

Good to Know: 
Free from sulphate 

Best Suited For: 
All skin types 

Ideal For: 
Men and women 

Product Form: 
Liquid 

Container Type: 
Tube 

Area of Application: 
Face 

Concern: 
Dull skin 

Directions for Use: 

- Apply an adequate amount of the face wash on wet face and neck 

- Gently work into a creamy lather by massaging in an upward circular motion while avoiding the eye area 

- Rinse off and pat dry 

Safety Information: 

- Read the label carefully before use 

- Store in a cool, dry place at a temperature not exceeding 25°C 

- Do not freeze 

- For external use only 

Quick Tips: 
Use twice a day for the best results.
