[Description)]
Ahaglow Advanced Skin Rejuvenating Face Wash cleanses the skin with its natural foaming ingredients and helps improve skin texture. This face care product is made with more than 80% purified water and contains plant-based natural surfactants that help moisturise the skin. The face wash is enriched with Aloe Vera and Vitamin E, which rejuvenates dry and dull skin and keeps the skin soft and hydrated. 

Key Ingredients: 
Purified Water, Acrylate Copolymer, Sodium Laureth Sulphate, Aloe Vera, Polysorbate 80, Sodium Cocoyl, Apple Amino Acid, Decyl Glucoside, Coloured Milicapsules, Glycolic Acid, Phenoxyethanol, Fragrance, Vitamin E Acetate, Potassium Hydroxide 

Key Benefits: 

- Rejuvenates the skin by encouraging the shedding of dead skin cells 

- Reduces the appearance of the number of wrinkles 

- Improves skin tone and texture 

- Unblocks and cleanses the pores 

Good to Know: 
Non-comedogenic, paraben-free, and contains fragrance. 

Best Suited For: 
Dull skin and clogged pores 

Product Form: 
Face wash gel 

Area of Application: 
Face 

Concern: 
Dead skin cells, skin texture, clogged pores, and wrinkles 

Directions for Use: 

- Wash the face with lukewarm water 

- Squeeze out the required quantity of the face wash gel on the palm and crush the milicapsules 

- Work up a lather and massage gently on the wet face and neck in an upward circular motion 

- Rinse with lukewarm water and pat dry with a soft towel 

Safety Information: 

- Read the label carefully before use 

- Store at a temperature not exceeding 25°C. Do not freeze 

- Keep out of reach of children 

Quick Tips: 
Use twice a day for the best results.
