# Metosartan 50 Tablet 

₹ 222.00 

About Metosartan 50 Tablet 

Metosartan 50 Tablet is used to treat hypertension (high blood pressure). Hypertension is a condition in which the force of the blood against the artery walls is too high. Symptoms include headache, dizziness, nosebleeds, changes in vision, chest pain, weakness, and dyspnea (shortness of breath). 

Metosartan 50 Tablet is a combination of Telmisartan and Metoprolol. This medicine relaxes the blood vessels and lowers the blood pressure. 

Not everyone who takes Metosartan 50 Tablet will experience these side effects. In some cases, you may experience common side effects, including headache, nausea, dizziness, weakness, stomach pain, and a slow heart rate. Most of these side effects do not require medical attention and will resolve gradually over time. 

You can take Metosartan 50 Tablet with or without food. It should be swallowed as a whole with a glass of water. Do not chew, bite, crush, or break it. Your doctor will advise you on how often you should take your tablets based on your medical condition. It is always important to complete your treatment course for better results. 

Inform your doctor if you are allergic to Metosartan 50 Tablet . Try not to stop taking it on your own as it may cause a sudden increase the blood pressure, chest pain or heart attack. Consult your doctor before starting Metosartan 50 Tablet if you are pregnant or breastfeeding. Metosartan 50 Tablet may cause dizziness, so drive only if you are alert. Avoid alcohol consumption with Metosartan 50 Tablet as it may cause increased dizziness. 

Side Effects of Metosartan 50 Tablet 

- Headcahe - Tiredness - Dizziness - Slow heart rate - Stomach pain - Nausea - Vomiting 

Uses of Metosartan 50 Tablet 

Metosartan 50 Tablet is used in the treatment of Hypertension (high blood pressure). The detailed use of Metosartan 50 Tablet is as follows: 

- Treatment of Hypertension: Metosartan 50 Tablet is used to treat high blood pressure by relaxing blood vessels and reducing heart rate, thereby lowering blood pressure and reducing the risk of cardiovascular complications. 

Directions for Use 

- Metosartan 50 Tablet can be taken with or without food, as advised by your doctor. - Follow your doctor’s instructions on the dosage and timing of this medication. Try to take it at the same time each day to help maintain steady levels of this medication in your blood. - Swallow Metosartan 50 Tablet as a whole with a glass of water. - Do not crush, chew, or break it. 

Medicinal Benefits 

- Metosartan 50 Tablet is a combination of two blood pressure-lowering medicines: telmisartan and metoprolol. - Telmisartan works by blocking the angiotensin II receptor, thereby relaxing the blood vessels and lowering the blood pressure. - Metoprolol helps to reduce blood pressure by allowing the blood to flow more smoothly. 

How Metosartan 50 Tablet Works 
Metosartan 50 Tablet is a combination of two high blood pressure-lowering medicines: Telmisartan and Metoprolol. Telmisartan belongs to the class of drugs called Angiotensin II receptor antagonists that works by blocking the angiotensin II receptor, relaxing the blood vessels and lowering the blood pressure. Metoprolol belongs to the class of drugs known as beta-blockers which reduces blood pressure by allowing the blood to flow more smoothly. Together, Metosartan 50 Tablet helps in reducing the raised blood pressure. 
Storage 
• Store in a cool and dry place away from sunlight. • Keep out of sight and reach of children. • Non cold chain – To be stored in cool places at 10ºC to 25ºC or below 30ºC. 
What if I have taken an overdose of Metosartan 50 Tablet 
Do not take more than the prescribed dose of Metosartan 50 Tablet as it may cause overdose. If you suspect you have taken overdose, please consult a doctor immediately. 
Drug Warnings 

- Do not take Metosartan 50 Tablet if you are allergic to telmisartan or metoprolol. - Inform your doctor if you are taking monoamine oxidase inhibitors, aliskiren, quinidine, disopyramide, or other medications that lower blood pressure. - Inform your doctor if you have kidney or liver diseases, biliary obstruction, heart problems, low blood pressure, diabetes, elevated potassium levels, blood circulation problems, asthma, COPD, metabolic acidosis, pheochromocytoma, slow heart rate, psoriasis, myasthenia gravis, or Prinzmetal angina. - Pregnant and breastfeeding women should consult their doctor before taking Metosartan 50 Tablet . - Metosartan 50 Tablet is not recommended for children below 18 years of age. - Metosartan 50 Tablet may cause dizziness and tiredness, so drive or operate heavy machinery only if you are alert. - Prolonged intake of Metosartan 50 Tablet can cause an altered potassium level. So, regular monitoring of potassium is advised. - It is always important to check the expiry of the medicine. Discard the medicine if it reaches the expiry date.
